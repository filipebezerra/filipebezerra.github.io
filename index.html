Dicionário de Biologia

An awesome dictionary for studying biology focused on high school classes

The Mini Dictionary of Biology from Editora W Mini Dictionaries Collection seeks to bring together in a single copy some of the most important concepts in biology. With this application the study of biology becomes fast and dynamic. Through research tool the user can have a quick access to entries can also add them to your favorites so causing it to create a customized list of the most important words.

For more information access the official download site of free version or the paid version.

  • Client: Editora W
